Ordering & Contact Info

Additional Considerations

  • $25 for each additional animal added to a single portrait
  • Additional portraits of individual animals available at base price
  • Prices are not affected by choice of black and white vs. color
  • Additional charges may apply for background scenery, text, and horse tack depending on complexity
  • For the best quality prints send your file to your favorite print shop or ask about ordering a physical print!

Please Include the Following Information When Ordering

  • Type of Commission
  • Number of animals to be included in the portrait
  • A Reference photo (or photos!) of the animals you wish to be included in the portrait
    ** The Higher quality photos the better! Try to choose photos with good lighting and your pet clearly in focus
  • Preferred Art Style (Black and White, Pencil Drawing, color preferences, etc.)
  • Any additional details that you wish to be included in the portrait
  • Back-stories about your pets and their personalities are always welcome and will help make your artwork extra special
